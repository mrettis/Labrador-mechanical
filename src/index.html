Gas fired furnaces:

Labrador Mechanical LLC can install a new gas furnace, or replace an existing gas furnace that has failed, using RUUD brand equipment with efficiencies ranging from 80% to 97%. 80% equipment is suitable for older homes with most masonry or metal chimneys or where the furnace is located in a space that could freeze.
90% and higher efficiency furnaces are a great choice where the existing flue is no longer usable.

Maintenance / Repair Services

Labrador Mechanical LLC will strive to provide you with an accurate diagnosis of the problem, a recommended corrective action and, where appropriate, an alternative course of action, including costs, parts availability, time necessary for completing the repair or major component replacement. Should we find that the repairs would be ineffective, short term or otherwise not the best course of action, we will explain and demonstrate if you wish, how and why we have arrived at that conclusion. Labrador Mechanical LLC does not and will not condemn a piece of equipment simply to sell you a new unit. We want to make our living the old fashioned way: honestly.
